### Usage
- **CTAs:** Sunset Gold #E8A849 with Navy text, or Coral #E07A5F
- **Dark backgrounds:** Midnight Navy #1B2838 (never pure black)
- **Light sections:** Morning Mist #F4F1EC
- **Links/accents:** Ocean Teal #34c5c5
- **Hover states:** Slightly darker/lighter variants of each color
- **Overall feel:** Warm, calm, premium — not harsh contrast
